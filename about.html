About Sunny Cafe

Sunny Cafe is a cozy neighborhood spot where friends and families gather to enjoy high-quality coffee and fresh pastries. Established in 2015, we pride ourselves on sourcing the finest beans from local roasters and baking our goods daily.

Our blue-themed interior creates a calming atmosphere, perfect for a quick espresso or a leisurely brunch. Join us and experience the warmth of Sunny Cafe!
